The leg raise is a strength training exercise to strengthen the rectus

abdominis muscle the internal and external oblique muscles.


Leg lifts or leg raises work the core as well as the hips and lower back,

making them a great move to master for improved overall strength.



HOW IT WORKS


Lie down on your back with one leg bent and one leg straight.

Next, perform a posterior pelvic tilt to protect your back from injury

With your toes pointed to the ceiling, keep your knee straight and lift your

up to about 45 degrees.
